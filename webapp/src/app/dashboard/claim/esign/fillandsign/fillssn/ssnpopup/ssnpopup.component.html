Social Security Number Required

The Unclaimed Property Department requires that you provide a social security number so they can process your claim.

How ClaimFound Handles Your SSN

  • ClaimFound never stores your SSN in its database directly, it is only kept on your signed claim form.
  • Once your claim is processed by the Unclaimed Property Department, we remove the form and your SSN from our platform.
  • During the short period of time where we store and transmit your claim form, we encrypt it at all times using bank-level security to avoid any anyone from getting access to it.

Learn more about how we protect your privacy on our Privacy Policy.

How To Process Your Claim Manually

If you don't want to provide your SSN to ClaimFound, you will have to manually process your claim through the state.

  • Google your state unclaimed property department website.
  • Find the unclaimed property search bar and look up your name and then find the claims you have selected in ClaimFound.
  • Research your state's instructions to determine what steps you need to take to get your money back.
  • Print your documentation and state claim form and then provide your SSN and sign the form.
  • Package all of your documents and mail them to the unclaimed property department.
  • Wait 2 - 4 weeks for confirmation from the unclaimed property department that your claim has been received
