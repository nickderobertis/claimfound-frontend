THE PROCESS
A few years later (in 2018), I remembered those checks and wondered what happened to them. After doing a quick search on ClaimFound, I saw 3 claims totaling around $1100 and it dawned on me: those must have been my old paychecks.
I immediately signed up and went through the whole process in around 30 minutes - it took me a little longer to find the supporting documentation than I thought. ClaimFound made it easy though, and about 6 weeks later I got my check for $1100.
