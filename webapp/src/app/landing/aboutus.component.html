Why We Started ClaimFound
In 2014, a close family friend learned that he had an unclaimed life insurance policy in his name, but no idea how to find it. Fast forward more than a year later, countless hours researching, multiple legal consultations, and bureaucratic red tape, he had finally gotten his money.
We wondered if there was an easier way and how many people had the same experience? No surprise - there wasn't and lots of people. That's when we decided to try and make the process easier.
