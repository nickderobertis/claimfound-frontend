At ClaimFound, we believe complete transparency is the only way to build genuine trust with our users and partners. This is especially true for how we gather and respect your personal information. To achieve our goal for transparency, we have written and designed this online privacy policy to empower and inform you about your privacy while on ClaimFound’s website.
YOUR DATA IS SECURE & PROTECTED
All data entered into the Site is encrypted using industry-standard SSL technology before being sent to our servers in an off-site data center. Our servers are supported by multiple levels of protection including firewalls, private subnets and multi-factor authentication.
YOUR DATA IS YOURS
You have control over how we share your data. We may need to share your information with our partners so they can perform requested services which allow us to operate the platform: for example, we pass your information to Unclaimed Property Departments so they can process your claims. You can opt out of these arrangements at any time by contacting us.
WE ARE COMMITTED TO FIGHTING FRAUD
Unclaimed Property is an industry surrounded by misinformation and skepticism. There are many reported cases of individuals who fraudulently try to claim other peoples money each year. To help ensure that the only person who can claim your unclaimed property is the rightful owner of that property - you - we have developed a process to verify your identity and to alert us if someone is fraudulently trying to claim your money.
